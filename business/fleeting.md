## Cash flow

- The amount of money that is coming in(profit) and going out(expense) the business

```
For early stage of business, it is important to have steady amount of cash flow. The amount of money going in as profit should be enough to cover the expenses from production, labor, and, etc.,.

If you have steady cash flow, it means you have enough money to cover expenses. Hence, your business is stable.
```

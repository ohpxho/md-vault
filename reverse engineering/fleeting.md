# Reverse Enginnering Notes

## Levels of Reverse Engineering

1. String: `xxd`(hex dump), `strings`(command)
2. Static Analysis: `strings`(command), `ida`(assembly & decompilation)
3. Dynamic Analysis: `gdb`
4. Symbolic Execution: `angr`(python lib)
5. Finding real bugs in real software: `binwalk`
